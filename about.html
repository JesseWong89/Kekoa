Who is founder of Kekoa?

Dominic Martin Lopez is currently the CEO and Founder of the Kekoa Veteran Foundation, which Kekoa means "the warrior" in Hawaiian. He is a Navy combat veteran who has served for 16 years and is a 3rd generation Navy SeaBee. As an Equipemnt Operator (E.O.) he was a combat support for the 1st Marine Division, search and rescue and emergency road clearing for Hurricane Katrina, combat contingency in Albania, and returned to Iraq as combat support with Seal Team 5. Dominis is also a certified combat skills instructor. He trained Seabees attached to Seal Team units, as well as Naval Amphibious units. His most notable deployments were Operation Iraqi Freedom, Operation Enduring Freedom, and Operation New Dawn. He currently resides with this son in Montebello, CA and volunteers in various communities throughout Los Angeles County. He works with children as well as homeless and mentally ill veterans.
